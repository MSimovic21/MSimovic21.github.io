Broadly speaking, my research seeks to understand the relationship between plants and their environment.

What are the physiological mechanisms linking climate change and yellow-cedar decline? What sorts of hydraulic and structural constraints have shaped axial xylem morphology? How can urban forests ameliorate the negative effects of climate change? These are just a few of the diverse array of topics which my academic research is focused on - see below for more information.
